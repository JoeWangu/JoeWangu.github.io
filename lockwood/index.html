About

LOCKWOOD AFRICA LTD is a Furniture shop that designs, crafts and sells unique Mahogany products. Long life furniture that remains a classic for centuries. At Lockwood Africa, we believe in creating the right atmosphere in a home with our unique products. Our main emphasis is on high quality, beautifully designed and crafted mahogany furniture that is a classic in every home. We believe in offering the best with no compromise on the quality.

What we offer

FREE DELIVERY

We offer free delivery within Nairobi and it's environs. We help with set up at your residence, and ensure everything is perfect before we leave. Any complaints or challenges can be communicated to the office for follow up.

100% MAHOGANY

Specializing in mahogany furniture that is unique, timeless and carefully crafted to cater to a quality conscious market. The mahogany we use is well seasoned, and with the right and proper care, can last for many years.

CUSTOMER SERVICE

At Lockwood, we like to take care of our customers, it was part of our mission when we started the business. We realize that our customers keep us going, and therefore, they deserve the best.
